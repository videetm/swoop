What's the core focus of your WHOOP-like app?

WHOOP tracks three pillars — pick the ones you want to build

A

Recovery Score

Daily readiness score based on HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep quality. The "how ready am I today?" number.

B

Strain / Exertion

Activity-based cardiovascular load throughout the day. How hard did you push your body?

C

Sleep Tracking

Sleep stages, sleep debt, time in bed vs asleep, sleep performance score.

D

HRV Trends

Heart rate variability over time — the gold standard metric for stress and recovery.

You can select multiple. The classic WHOOP experience is A + B + C together.
